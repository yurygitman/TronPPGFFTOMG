> LIVE DATA ONLY — no simulation. Wiring: Signal → GPIO1 (A0/D0) · + → 3V3 · − → GND. Upload PulseSensor_WebSerial_Plus.ino (streams signal,bpm,ibi,beat,amp,inside), close any Serial Monitor, then Connect. Chrome / Edge / Brave on desktop only. Nothing on this page moves until real samples arrive; on disconnect or signal loss every panel blanks to NOT CONNECTED / NO SIGNAL.

> Inhale/exhale is anchored to respiratory sinus arrhythmia (heart rate rises on inhalation — the one physiologically fixed sign); RIIV baseline polarity is auto-calibrated against it. Everything here is derived in the browser from one optical sensor — a learning/biofeedback tool, not a medical device.
